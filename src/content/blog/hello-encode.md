Encode is the category for learning that sticks. Every AI concept, paper, or technique I study gets a location in a memory palace — a concrete image in a specific place — so it's retrievable months later, not just familiar for a week. The method is old; the material is current.

Posts here will cover what I'm encoding, how I'm encoding it, and whether the palace held up when I came back to test it. If a concept didn't survive retrieval practice, that gets written down too.
