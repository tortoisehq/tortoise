Forge is the workshop. Everything in this category is about making things and putting them in front of people. Code, tools, side projects, the small experiments that turn into bigger ones. If a post lives here, something got built — or something got broken in a way worth writing down.

The bias is toward shipping over polishing. A weekend prototype that runs counts. A half-finished idea that taught me something counts. The point is to keep the cycle short: build, write, learn, build again. Twenty-four months is a lot of cycles if you don't waste them.

Expect honest write-ups: what I tried, what worked, what didn't, and what I'd do differently next time. No victory laps for things that aren't working yet.
