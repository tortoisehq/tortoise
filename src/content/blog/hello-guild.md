Guild is for learning out loud. The medieval guilds figured out something useful: the best way to get good at a craft is to do it next to people who are also trying to get good at it, and to talk about it while you work. That's the spirit here.

Posts in this category will cover whatever I'm currently studying — books, papers, conversations, courses — and what I'm taking from them. Not summaries. Notes from someone trying to actually use the material, where the friction is, where my prior model was wrong.

If you read something here that's incomplete or outright incorrect, that's a feature, not a bug. I'd rather post the half-formed version and get corrected than wait until I'm sure and post nothing.
